OSCEtimer.app

The OSCE Timer is a free, web-based synchronized countdown timer based on peerJS.

All users are connected to a synchronized timer via a peer-to-peer connection using the ID provided by the administrator.

You can choose to have one of two roles. An administrator controls the countdown duration and the starting or stopping of the timer. A listener listens to the time signal broadcast from the administrator. A peer-to-peer connection ensures that the timers on different connected devices are synchronized.
